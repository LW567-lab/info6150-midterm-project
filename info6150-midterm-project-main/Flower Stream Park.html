Flower Stream Park

Explore the Beauty of Flower Stream Park

Flower Stream Park (Huaxi Park) forms the center of the Flower Stream Scenic Area.

Blending natural hills and rivers, idyllic scenery with ethnic features, it has gained the name of "Back Garden of Guiyang City".

It is located in the south suburb of Guiyang City, Guizhou Province. It covers an area of about 825 mu (about 136 acres), of which about 300 mu (about 49 acres) is water area.
